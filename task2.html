About Pavel Durov

Born 1984 — living • Founder of VK & Telegram

Pavel Durov (born 1984) is an entrepreneur known for building large-scale social and communication platforms. He founded the VK social network in 2006 and later co-founded the Telegram messenger in 2013. Telegram emphasizes speed, security, and freedom of expression and has grown to hundreds of millions of users worldwide. Durov is known for a minimalist product philosophy and a strong stance on digital independence.

Key Events (Timeline)

  1. 1984 — Born in Leningrad (now Saint Petersburg).
  2. 2006 — Launched the VK social network.
  3. 2013 — Co-founded the Telegram messenger.
  4. 2014 — Left VK and focused on Telegram.
  5. 2010s → 2020s — Telegram reached hundreds of millions of users globally.

Inventions & Contributions

  • Created VK — one of the largest social networks in the CIS.
  • Launched Telegram — a fast, privacy-oriented messenger with cloud sync.
  • Advocates digital freedom and user privacy.
  • Product approach: minimalist design and performance focus.
  • Supports developers via open APIs and a rich bot/client ecosystem.
